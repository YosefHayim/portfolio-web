Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Audio Transcriber ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension. Audio Transcriber is developed and operated by Yosef Hayim Sabag.

Google API Data Disclosure

Audio Transcriber uses Google sign-in through Chrome Identity so you can authenticate your account and access subscription status. Audio Transcriber's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Information We Collect

Audio Transcriber may collect your account profile information when you sign in, audio files or microphone recordings you choose to transcribe, generated transcripts, transcription history, extension settings, usage/quota information, and subscription status. Payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy; we do not store credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to provide audio transcription, save and display your transcript history, enforce free and Pro usage limits, authenticate your account, manage subscription access, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

3. Audio and Transcript Processing

Audio content you submit is sent to the Audio Transcriber backend and transcription service only for the purpose of generating transcripts and related metadata. We do not sell your audio, transcripts, or account data, and we do not use them for unrelated advertising or creditworthiness purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Extension settings, session state, and transcript history may be stored in your browser and in our backend systems as needed to provide the service. We use reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information and limit access to what is necessary to operate Audio Transcriber.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Account and subscription records are retained while your account is active or as required for legal, billing, and security purposes. You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting us, and we will remove eligible personal data within 30 days.
