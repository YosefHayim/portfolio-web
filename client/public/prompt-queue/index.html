Features

🚀 Queue Management

Add unlimited prompts and let PromptQueue automatically submit them to Gemini while you work. Set drip-feed timing, pause/resume anytime, and watch results come in automatically. No more manual copy-pasting.

🎨 Bulk Image Generation

Generate dozens or hundreds of images in one batch. Choose between Gemini 2.0 Flash or Imagen 3, and watch as images are automatically created and downloaded to your computer. Perfect for product photography, design exploration, and content creation.

🌐 Bulk Translation & Optimization

Translate your prompts instantly to 8+ languages for global campaigns. Optimize prompts across multiple AI providers (Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude) to get better results. Localize content at scale in minutes instead of days.

⚙️ Automation & Scheduling

Schedule queue processing for specific times (perfect for overnight runs), enable automatic smart retry on failures, and set realistic timing between submissions to avoid rate limits. Set it and forget it.

Save your favorite prompts as reusable templates and organize them into color-coded folders. Build a library of brand-consistent prompts and instantly reuse them for similar tasks. Perfect for teams and recurring workflows.
